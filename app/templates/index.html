Welcome to Reci-Pic

Reci-pic is an all-in-one virtual kitchen assistant for students and young professionals.

Get started

Opening up a fridge and not knowing what to make?

Reci-Pic: Easy access to recipes right from your fridge.

Efficiency

The main goal of this app is to accommodate busy people on the go. Our hope is that users can go from opening their fridge to viewing recipes in seconds. People have busy lives and are constantly working. This app will make it easy for people who need easy ways to create recipes for themselves with the ingredients already in their kitchen.

Personalization

Even without any fancy additional features, being able to view recipes based on what a user currently has in their fridge gives them a sense of personalization that can accommodate their available ingredients and different diets.

Accessible

Our app allows users to generate recipes through their mobile devices. It is easy to access and user-friendly. Users not only can learn new recipes but also advance themselves in cooking!


Our app timeline.

01

Deploy our image classifier on the website.

Below you can test out our ingredient classification model that can identify different types of produce including vegetables, dairy products, meat, grains, and fruits.

02

Create Visual App

Our next step is to combine our machine learning model and searching feature into a easily accessible format through android/iOS. Our goal is that the user will be able to access the camera through their app and take a picture to have our scanner classify the ingredients.

03

Implement Recipe Generator

From there, we hope to create a list of food ingredients and then use that list to search through the database of recipes and output those can feature these ingredients.

Try it Out!

Keep in mind that this is just a first step for our app. Our model currently displays the detected ingredients in the image. Our next steps are implementing live scanning and recipe recommendations with the detected ingredients.
